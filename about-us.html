About Us

Meet the visionary leaders driving innovation and excellence in Miami's Design District

April Sabral

Executive Director

April Sabral brings over two decades of transformational leadership experience to the Miami Design District Chamber of Commerce. As a visionary executive, she has consistently driven organizational excellence across diverse industries, from luxury retail to cutting-edge AI technology.

Her unique blend of strategic acumen and operational expertise makes her the ideal leader to guide our chamber's mission of fostering innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth within Miami's premier design destination.

April's commitment to empowering businesses and building meaningful partnerships aligns perfectly with our vision of establishing the Design District as a globally recognized hub of creativity and commerce.

Board of Directors

Lubna Najjar

Board President
Lubna Najjar is an accomplished entrepreneur, brand developer, and fashion designer, with over a decade of experience in the fashion and brand development industries. As the founder of IL Moda Brand, LLC (2012-present) and Her Denim, LLC, she has played a pivotal role in building and launching over 150 fashion brands, focusing on contemporary and influencer-led labels. Lubna was the curator and developer of the official Fashion District in downtown Columbus, Ohio, showcasing her ability to shape and drive industry initiatives on a large scale. Lubna now serves on the Board of Directors for the Design District Chamber of Commerce in Miami, FL, and BBB of South East Florida serving 240,000 businesses.
