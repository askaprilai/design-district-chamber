Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Ask April AI different from other leadership training?

Ask April AI combines 30+ years of real-world retail and hospitality experience with AI technology. You get practical, floor-tested strategies that work in your industry, not generic leadership advice.

How does the AI coaching work?

Our AI coach is trained on April's decades of experience managing teams in retail and hospitality. You can ask questions 24/7 and get personalized advice for your specific leadership challenges.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts or hidden fees. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

What's included in the 30+ online courses?

Our courses cover everything from difficult conversations and team motivation to sales coaching and customer service excellence. All content is bite-sized and designed for busy managers.

How do the Live Office Hours work?

Every week, April hosts live Q&A sessions where you can ask questions directly and connect with other managers facing similar challenges. Sessions are recorded if you can't attend live.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Builder plan comes with a 30-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Experience the full platform before you commit.

What if I need help for my entire team?

Our Scaler and GROW plans are designed for teams and multiple locations. You'll get employee onboarding tools, progress tracking, and the ability to create private communities for your organization.

Do you offer consulting services?

Yes! If you need more personalized help, April offers one-on-one consulting for specific challenges or strategic planning. Contact us to discuss your needs.
