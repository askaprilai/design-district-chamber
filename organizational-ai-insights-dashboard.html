🔒 Privacy & Data Controls

Anonymous Data Collection
All individual responses are anonymized before aggregation. No personal identifiers are stored.
Department-Level Insights
Show aggregated data by department (minimum 5 responses required).
Anonymous Quote Sharing
Share anonymized employee feedback to provide context for data trends.
Data Retention
Automatically delete individual response data after 12 months (aggregated insights retained).

🛡️ Privacy Commitment

We prioritize psychological safety:

  • Individual responses are never shared with managers or leadership
  • Department breakdowns only show when 5+ people participate
  • All quotes are anonymized and cannot be traced to individuals
  • Data is used solely for improving organizational AI readiness
  • Employees can request their data to be excluded at any time
