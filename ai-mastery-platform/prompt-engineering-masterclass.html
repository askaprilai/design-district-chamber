Lesson 1

The Foundation Problem

Why 90% of people get terrible AI results (and how to fix it)

❌ What Most People Do
Write a marketing email

Result: Generic, boring email that could be for any company selling anything. Sounds like it was written by AI.

✅ What AI Experts Do
You are a direct-response copywriter with 15 years of experience in SaaS marketing. Write a marketing email for our project management software targeting overwhelmed small business owners who are currently using spreadsheets to manage their projects. Context: - Our software reduces project chaos by 80% - Target audience: 10-50 employee companies - Pain point: Teams miss deadlines, lose track of tasks, clients get frustrated - Our unique benefit: Simplicity - setup in 5 minutes, no training needed Tone: Conversational, empathetic, solution-focused Length: 150-200 words Include: Clear subject line, specific benefit, soft call-to-action Make it feel like it's written by a human who genuinely understands their frustration.

Result: Compelling, specific email that speaks directly to the target audience's pain points and gets responses.

🧠 The 4 Pillars of Effective Prompting

🎭

1. Role & Persona

Tell AI exactly who it should be. Expert consultant? Creative director? Data analyst?

"You are a senior business consultant with 20 years of experience in retail operations..."
🎯

2. Context & Constraints

Provide specific background, limitations, and parameters for the task.

"Our target audience is tech-savvy millennials. Budget: $50K. Deadline: 2 weeks. Must comply with GDPR..."
📝

3. Task & Format

Be crystal clear about what you want and exactly how you want it delivered.

"Create a 5-point bulleted list with specific actionable steps. Each point should be 1-2 sentences..."
🎨

4. Tone & Style

Specify the voice, feeling, and personality you want in the output.

"Tone: Professional but warm, like a trusted advisor. Avoid jargon. Write like you're explaining to a smart friend..."

🛠️ Quick Practice: Transform This Bad Prompt

Bad Prompt: "Help me with social media"

Using the 4 pillars, how would you improve this? Think about what's missing...

Hint: What role should AI play? What's your business? What specific help do you need? What's your style?

Lesson 2

5 Game-Changing Techniques

Copy-paste templates that instantly improve your results

🎭

1. Role Playing

Make AI act as different experts to get specialized insights.

You are [SPECIFIC EXPERT ROLE]. [YOUR REQUEST] Respond as this expert would, using their knowledge, perspective, and communication style.
🔄

2. Chain of Thought

Ask AI to show its reasoning process for better, more accurate results.

[YOUR QUESTION OR REQUEST] Think through this step-by-step: 1. First, consider... 2. Then, analyze... 3. Finally, conclude... Show your reasoning for each step.
📚

3. Few-Shot Examples

Give AI examples of what you want to get consistent, high-quality outputs.

I need you to [TASK]. Here are examples of what I want: Example 1: [GOOD EXAMPLE] Example 2: [ANOTHER GOOD EXAMPLE] Now create something similar for: [YOUR SPECIFIC REQUEST]
😈

4. Devil's Advocate

Get AI to challenge your ideas and find weaknesses you missed.

I'm considering [YOUR IDEA/PLAN]. Play devil's advocate: What could go wrong? What am I not considering? What are the potential downsides? Be brutally honest and help me strengthen this idea.
🎯

5. Iterative Refinement

Start broad, then narrow down with follow-up prompts for precision.

Round 1: [BROAD REQUEST] Round 2: "That's good, now make it more [SPECIFIC ADJUSTMENT]" Round 3: "Perfect, now adapt it for [SPECIFIC CONTEXT]"
🔍

Bonus: The 5-Why Technique

Dig deeper into any AI response by asking "why" five times.

[AI gives you an answer] You: "Why is that the case?" [AI explains] You: "Why does that happen?" [Keep going 5 times for deep insights]

🚀 Real Business Example

❌ Basic Approach
Help me improve our customer service
✅ Expert Approach
You are a customer experience consultant who has helped 100+ SaaS companies reduce churn and increase satisfaction. Our situation: - B2B software company, 500 customers - Current response time: 4 hours average - Customer satisfaction: 7.2/10 - Main complaints: slow responses, generic answers - Team: 3 support agents, limited budget Analyze this step-by-step: 1. What are the top 3 root causes of our issues? 2. What's the highest-impact improvement we could make with minimal budget? 3. How would you prioritize improvements over the next 90 days? Give specific, actionable recommendations with expected outcomes.

Advanced Prompt Engineering

Power-user techniques for complex business challenges

🧩 Multi-Step Prompt Strategies

🎪

The Perspective Circus

Get multiple expert viewpoints on the same problem.

I need to solve [PROBLEM]. Give me perspectives from: 1. A conservative risk-averse executive 2. An innovative startup founder 3. A data-driven analyst 4. A customer experience expert What would each say? Where do they agree/disagree?
🏗️

The Building Blocks Method

Break complex tasks into smaller, manageable pieces.

I need to [COMPLEX TASK]. Let's break this down: Step 1: Help me outline the major components Step 2: I'll ask you to detail each component Step 3: We'll put it all together Step 4: We'll review and refine Start with Step 1: What are the major components?
🎲

The Scenario Planner

Explore different "what if" scenarios to prepare for uncertainty.

I'm planning [YOUR INITIATIVE]. Help me scenario plan: Best case scenario: Everything goes perfectly Most likely scenario: Normal challenges occur Worst case scenario: Major obstacles arise For each scenario: 1. What would happen? 2. How should I prepare? 3. What early warning signs should I watch for?
🔬

The Research Assistant

Turn AI into your personal research and analysis team.

Act as my research assistant for [TOPIC]. Phase 1: Help me identify the key questions I should be asking Phase 2: Suggest research approaches and sources Phase 3: Help me analyze and synthesize findings Phase 4: Draw actionable conclusions Let's start with Phase 1: What key questions should I explore?
